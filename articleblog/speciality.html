The Speciality In Us

Uddhav Thakur October 24, 2020
Nature is the collection of all the physical structures that are found on the earth including water, soil, and air. All the living beings present on the earth are a part of nature. Even we the human beings are inculcated inside this. Nature has made all the living beings on this earth with different features. For example, It has given wild animals a huge amount of strength so that they can hunt others for their living. In the same way, plants are given the peculiarity of preparing their own food without moving their hands or body with the help of the other components of the sun such as the rays of the Sun, the water from the oceans, the soil from the earth, etc.

Now you might be thinking about ourselves i.e. the humans and most probably you have guessed it also. So, nature has given us the power of thinking. We can efficiently utilize our brain. Some of them might say that we have more developed brain than the others but it might not be so. Actually, we can utilize our mind in a nicer way which has made us dominant over the other creatures. You know, there are different types of rules made by nature which we are unaware of but still practicing. The things which are not able to move, express, etc are dominant in terms of their number and size on this Earth which are called the plants pr more precisely the producers. Whereas, the things which can move, express their feelings with each other, and can hunt are less in number. This has maintained the balance between living beings a lot. 



Similarly, nature has given the organisms living in a cold area, a big/thick fur to protect themselves whereas the animals who live in hot areas have less fur. But what about us? We, humans, do not have any specific area to live, any specific temperature to live in, why? The answer is, our mind. We can utilize our brains, so we have made clothes to protect ourselves according to different temperatures. The animals which have huge physical strength can be killed by themselves although they have small and tiny creatures called bacteria and viruses on their body. But when we are attacked by big animals like lions and tigers we can protect ourselves using our inventions called ammunition which is operated according to our brain. The animals like dogs, cats, cows, etc have many viruses on their body, but also nothing happens to them. But if the same happens to us, then we die as soon as we are exposed to it. Why has nature not given us the power to restrain it? The answer of course, is our brain. 





Nature has given us a very special quality and thus today we can rule upon others, but we are not utilizing our brain properly. Instead, we are killing nature by harming all those organisms and trees along with the water resources and the soil using different activities. This has led to an imbalance in our nature. Different organisms have got extinct from this earth due to anthropological activities. Global warming, climate change, melting of the glaciers, deforestation, extinction of species, high temperature of the earth, the formation of viruses and bacteria, forest fire, etc. are the consequences of it.

But it's not too late, we can still change these effects by positively utilizing our brain. When we all will think positively about nature, then, of course, it will turn clean and green like it was in its initial stage. Thus, let's do all those activities that promote the environment around us, and instead of ruling nature in a crucial way, let's rule it positively i.e. by being a leader instead of a boss. Thank you!!!
