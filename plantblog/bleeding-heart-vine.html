BLEEDING HEART VINE:

Binayak Jha October 25, 2020

#) BLEEDING HEART VINE:

                                                        


SCIENTIFIC NAME: Clerodendrum thomsoniae

#)INTRODUCTION

This plant is also known as glory bower or tropical bleeding heart. It is a subtropical vine that wraps its tendrils around other tendrils or other support. This plant is known for its shiny green leaves and beautiful crimson and white bloom. Its length reaches about 15 feet(4.5 meters).

#) HOW TO TAKE CARE OF BLEEDING HEART VINE?

  1. Water the plant frequently during dry weather.
  2. This plant requires completely moist soil.
  3. The plant should be given frequent fertilization to supply the nutrients required to produce the flower.
  4. Prevent it from the bugs which can damage its leaves.

#) HOW TO PROPAGATE BLEEDING HEART VINE?

Bleeding heart vine is easy to propagate by cuttings. The tip of the plant is taken for propagation. It is kept in water for about 2 weeks. After the roots come out it can be kept in soil for further growth.
