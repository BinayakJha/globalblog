SPIDER PLANT

Binayak Jha October 22, 2020

SPIDER PLANT:


                                                        


SCIENTIFIC NAME:    Chlorophytum comosum 

COMMON NAME:   SPIDER PLANT

#)INTRODUCTION:

This plant is an indoor plant. It is well-known for its leaf color. This plant leaf is greenish-white color.

This plant is known as a spider plant because of its leaf which comes from its center point. It is a species of perennial flowering plants. This is an office plant. 

#)BENEFITS OF SPIDER PLANT:

  1. The plant produces oxygen which purifies the air of your house.
  2. It purifies the air by absorbing carbon monoxide, formaldehyde, etc.
  3. They are non-toxic, and hence safe for children's and your pets

#)HOW TO TAKE CARE OF SPIDER PLANT?

  1. This plant can be kept in direct sunlight or in indirect sunlight also.
  2. Keep it well watered in its growing seasons.
  3.  It can bear the average room temperature.
  4. The baby plant can be kept in moist soil to grow its strong roots.

#)HOW TO PROPAGATE SPIDER PLANT?   

  1. The first way to propagate it is by just cutting its baby and keeping it in moist soil.
  2. The second way to propagate it is by keeping the baby plant in wet cotton or tissue paper.
  3. The third way is by keeping the baby plant end part in water.
