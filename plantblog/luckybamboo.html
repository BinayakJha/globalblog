Lucky Bamboo

Binayak Jha October 21, 2020

#)LUCKY BAMBOO:

                                                            
                                                      Photo by Toni Cuenca from Pexels

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Dracaena sanderiana

COMMON NAME: LUCKY BAMBOO

#)INTRODUCTION:

Lucky bamboo is one of the famous indoor plants. It was first seen in China and later on, it was used by every country.

 This plant can be grown both in water and soil. The plant can be propagated easily. This plant is believed to bring luck to the peoples who keep on their desks.

 The plant is known for its beautiful structures. The plant does not need much space and can be kept on the desk or other places in the room.

#)HOW TO TAKE CARE OF LUCKY BAMBOO?

  1. The plant needs clean water if kept in water and the plant needs moist soil if kept in soil for its growth.
  2. The plant should be watered every 2 days. 
  3. The plant can be grown in any shape
  4. The plant needs indirect sunlight. 


#)HOW TO PROPAGATE LUCKY BAMBOO?

This plant is easy to propagate. The plant can be propagated by cutting bamboo into 2or3 parts or it can be propagated by cutting the end part(from where the leaves come out) of leaves.

 And just keep it in water for some weeks. After roots start to come out keep it in the soil or if you want you can keep it in water also.
