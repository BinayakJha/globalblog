SNAKE PLANT

Binayak Jha October 21, 2020

   #)SNAKE PLANT:

                                                 
                                                         

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Sansevieria

#) INTRODUCTION:

    This plant is also known as a mother tolerant plant because it can survive in any weather condition. If we don't water it for a week or month it can survive. The plant likes dry soil but if you water it every day then also it can survive. It mostly likes indirect sunlight but can survive in any condition. This plant is also an air purifier, it helps in removing toxins like formaldehyde and benzene. This plant is a perfect indoor/house plant.

#) HOW TO TAKE CARE OF SNAKE PLANT?

  1. Put them in indirect sunlight for proper growth.
  2. Don't water them too much as they like dry soil.
  3. Little fertilizer can be used if the plants are in a pot.

#)HOW TO PROPAGATE SNAKE PLANT?

It's easy to propagate a snake plant. If it will have proper growth it will give many baby snake plant which you can afterward takeout and grow it in another pot. You can also cut leaves into parts and keep it in the soil after some months it will grow roots and a small baby snake plant will be grown.
