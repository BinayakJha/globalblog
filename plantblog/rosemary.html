ROSEMARY:

Binayak Jha October 24, 2020

#)ROSEMARY:

                                                      
                                                   
           SCIENTIFIC NAME:   Salvia rosmarinus   
           NORMAL NAME    : ROSEMARY

#)INTRODUCTION:

Rosemary, a herb whose botanical name is Rosmarinus officinalis.Its leaves are like a spiny throne. Its leaves are used in tea for the taste and are also healthy for human beings. It is non-toxic and does not harm any animals. This plant also purifies the air and gives a nice smell. Rosemary is from the family of 'mint'.

#)HOW TO TAKE CARE OF ROSEMARY?

  1. Water Rosemary plant throughout the growing season. But prevent overwatering.
  2. Cut some parts of the plant so that it won't get lanky(ungrateful thin and tall).
  3. Grow the plant indoors in the winter season.
  4. Keep it near the kitchen window as it is used in most of the cooking products.

#)HOW TO PROPAGATE ROSEMARY PLANT?

You can grow rosemary plants by cutting the younger stem and keeping it in soil by adding honey on the lower part. After some weeks the roots start to come and you can exchange the plant into a bigger pot.
