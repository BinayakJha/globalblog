#)ZANZIBAR GEM:

                                                                 


SCIENTIFIC NAME: Zamioculcas

COMMON NAME: Zuzu, ZZ, Aroid, Emerald Palm Plant                                                       

INTRODUCTION:

This plant is a poisonous plant that can be harmful to both animals and human beings. This plant comes from the family of aroid plants.

This plant does not need much water to grow and needs less/indirect sunlight to grow. The whole part of the plant is poisonous

This plant was found a couple of decades ago by Dutch nurseries in South Africa. They distributed this plant in every person's home and it became famous in the whole world.

This plant also purifies the air of the home(from NASA). This plant helps in removing harmful toxins like xylene, benzene. So I prefer you to keep it in your house.

 

#)WHY IS ZZ PLANT POISONOUS?

This question is asked by many people. And I had also got this question in my head.

 After getting some information from google and many books. I found that these plants contain Calcium Oxalate(a crystal with sharp edges). So whenever you handle this plant wash your hands to prevent skin irritation.

 #) IN HOW MANY DAYS INTERVAL DOES THIS PLANT NEEDS WATER?
Since these plants like dry soil, you can water this plant in an interval of 7to 12 days. Don't water too much, as it may create problems in the plant. 

The best way to know if this plant needs water is to poke your finger in the soil about 2 inches, if the soil is moist it doesn't need water, but if it is dry it needs water.

#)HOW TO TAKE CARE OF ZZ PLANT?
  1. Don't water this plant daily because it likes dry soil.
  2. Keep this plant in indirect sunlight.
  3. Hardly keep fertilizer in this plant.
  4. keep this plant in a medium-sized pot.

#) HOW TO PROPAGATE ZZ PLANT?

This plant is easy to propagate. First, you need to cut a young stem of this plant.

 After that keep well-cleaned sand in a pot.

 After that remove 2 - 3 pairs of leaves from the bottom and keep it in water.

 After a month the roots will come out and keep it in another pot for its future growth.
