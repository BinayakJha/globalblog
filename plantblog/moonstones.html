MOONSTONES PLANT

Binayak Jha November 2, 2020

#) MOONSTONES PLANT:


                                              

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Pachyphytum Oviferum

#)INTRODUCTION:

We can see many plants in our surrounding which are green in color. But this plant can either be Blueish-purple or a faded blue-green. It is also known as sugared almond. Their leaves are egg-shaped. Moonstones are endemic to Mexico. 

#) HOW TO TAKE CARE OF MOONSTONES PLANT?

  1. Moonstones can survive well both in full and partial sunlight.
  2. Keep it inside the home when the cold season set in.
  3. These plants should be watered once a week or month as it likes dry soil.but in winter it needs more water as this is its growing season.
  4. It should be kept in the soil which drains first enough. 

#) HOW TO PROPAGATE MOONSTONES PLANT?

  1. Cut a young leaf from the center.
  2. Allow it some time for the cut part to callous-usually a day is enough.
  3. Place the leaf into a slightly moist well-draining potting mix.
  4. Wait until a new plant with roots has come in it or not.
