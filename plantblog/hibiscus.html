HIBISCUS

Binayak Jha November 1, 2020

#)HIBISCUS:

                                           

  SCIENTIFIC NAME:  Hibiscus Rosa-Sinensis            

#)INTRODUCTION:

The leaves are often smooth or covered with plant hair. The flower can be borne in clusters and can last only a single day. The stamens are typically fused in the tube. They are mostly cultivated for their beautiful flower and are native to warm temperatures. Their flower is also used for medicinal purposes.
  

#) HOW TO TAKE CARE OF HIBISCUS?

  1. They require well-drained soil.  
  2. They do not tolerate wet soil.  
  3. They do need watering once a day.     
  4. They need fertilizer to get nutrients for growing flowers.                                                         

#)HOW TO PROPAGATE HIBISCUS?

The propagation of hibiscus is very easy. You just need a younger healthy branch. The stem should be cut into the shape of half T. Then the stem lower part of the stem should be mixed with rooting hormone and kept in moist soil. After some weeks the root is developed.
