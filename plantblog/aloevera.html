ALOEVERA

Binayak Jha October 27, 2020

#)ALOE VERA:



                                                     

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Aloe barbadensis miller

NORMAL NAME:  Ghreet kumari

Aloe vera has been known for its healing purposes for about 6000 years ago. In the early days, the plant was known as a " plant of immortality".These days aloe is used as a raw material in industries. Its juices are used in cosmetic products and also for personal health.
These aloe vera products are very beneficial for human beings. Its gel is very useful for health.


#)HOW TO TAKE CARE OF ALOE VERA?

  1. You can place it in direct sunlight, indirect sunlight.
  2. You should water it once in 3 weeks.
  3. keep the aloe vera near the kitchen window for use in the kitchen.

#)HOW TO PROPAGATE ALOE VERA?

The propagation of aloe vera is very easy. At first, cut a leaf of a plant and keep it in moist soil and after some weeks the roots start to come and a new aloe vera is formed.
