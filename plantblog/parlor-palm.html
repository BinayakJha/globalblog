Parlor Palm

Binayak Jha October 29, 2020

#)PARLOR PALM:

                                                          
                                             
                                         

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Chamaedorea Elegans

#)INTRODUCTION:

This plant has been used as an indoor plant for many years. This plant is a small, compact usually single-trunked plant. This plant can handle lower temperatures and grows attractive clumps. These plants are grown in an attractive pot to enhance the beauty of your room. 
The parlor palm is non-toxic for animals and humans.

#)HOW TO TAKE CARE OF PARLOR PALM?

  1. It prefers the area with high humidity.
  2. The soil around it should be moist.
  3. You can water freely in summer but in winter reduce water in winter.
  4. The soil should remain moist and well-drained.


#)HOW TO PROPAGATE  PARLOR PALM?

The parlor palm gives seed from which you can grow a new parlor plant. And before growing the plant the seeds should be soaked in water.
