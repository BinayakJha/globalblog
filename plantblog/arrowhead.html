ARROWHEAD PLANT

Binayak Jha October 20, 2020

#)ARROWHEAD-PLANT:

                                                                
 

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Syngonium podophyllum

COMMON NAME: Arrowhead Plant

INTRODUCTION:
This plant is well-known for its arrow shape. This plant is famous for its greenish-white color. 
The plant is toxic for pets. This plant can be grown in every type of light.
 And it does not need much big pot to grow. This plant should not be kept in direct sunlight as it will make the leaves dry. 

Since this plant is toxic, it should be kept away from your pets and humans also. 

*) WHAT TO DO IF YOUR ARROWHEAD PLANT LEAF ARE TURNING YELLOW? 
                                        The main reason why your arrowhead plant leaf is turning yellow is because of not getting enough water or getting too much water. So the best way to prevent its leaves from 
being yellow is by watering it in an interval of 3 or 4 days.

*)CAN WE GROW ARROWHEAD PLANT IN WATER?

                                       From my side, it's not better to grow it in water. You can find the information on google. Since this plant is the plant that needs less water to grow, there is a high chance of dying it in water. So I prefer you to grow it in soil.

#)HOW TO TAKE CARE OF ARROWHEAD PLANT?

                                      

  1. This plant does not need much water to grow so water it in an interval of 3 or 4 days.
  2. The pot of this plant should be well drainable to prevent it from storing unwanted water.
  3. The plant should be trimmed to make it bushy.
  4. Do not keep this plant in direct sunlight. Keep it in indirect sunlight or a less bright place.
 #)HOW TO PROPAGATE ARROWHEAD PLANT? 

The plant can be propagated by cutting its younger stem and keeping it in water or soil. I prefer this plant to propagate by cutting in the younger stem and keeping it in soil.
