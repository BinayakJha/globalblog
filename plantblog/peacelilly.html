PEACE LILY:

                                         

                                                   

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Spathiphyllum

COMMON NAME: Peace lily

#)INTRODUCTION:

Peace Lily is a very popular indoor plant which is the best air purifier. The peace lily is a plant often send in sympathy when someone in the family dies. 

It is also a symbol of peace and hope. Its flower is the reason for its name. The peace lily is a toxic plant so it should not be eaten by animals.


#)HOW TO TAKE CARE OF PEACE LILY?

  1. Keep the soil moist. Do not overwater to this plant.
  2. They enjoy high humidity. So, place their pot in a moist tray.
  3. They are tropical plants so keep them in the sun.
  4. Since they are not heavy feeders, keep fertilizer occasionally.

#)HOW TO PROPAGATE PEACE LILLY?

  1. Divide your peace lily just before and after plant blossoms.
  2. Always use room temperature water to water it.
  3. You can also propagate lilys in water.
