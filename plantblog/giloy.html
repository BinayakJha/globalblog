HEART-LEAVED MOONSEED(GILOY):

Binayak Jha October 23, 2020

#)HEART-LEAVED MOONSEED:

                                                             



SCIENTIFIC NAME: Tinospora cordifolia

COMMON NAME: GILOY

#)INTRODUCTION:

This plant is mostly used in ayurvedic for making medicine. This plant's leaf is in the shape of a heart.

 Both leaf and stem are used in medicine. This plant needs support to grow taller. The plant juice can be taken to increase immunity power. The plants can be grown anywhere.   

#)BENEFITS OF GILOY:

  1. It boosts your immunity to fight against any virus.
  2. It is effective against respiratory infection.
  3. Improves digestion
  4. It can help in controlling fever.
  5. It can also be used as an energy booster.

#)HOW TO TAKE CARE OF GILOY?

  1. Since it is a climber plant we should grow this plant near a big tree like mango, neem(best).
  2. The giloy which is found in the neem tree has more medicinal value.
  3. It needs medium water and needs more light.
  4. It needs normal soil(not too dry and not too wet)

#)HOW TO PROPAGATE GILOY?

It's very easy to propagate this plant. You can just cut a young stem of this plant and keep it in soil and water it.
