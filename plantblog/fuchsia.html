FUCHSIA(KRISHNA KALI)

Binayak Jha October 21, 2020

#) FUCHSIA:

                                               
                                             

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Phanasitti
COMMON NAME(NEPALI): KRISHNA KALI


#) INTRODUCTION:

This plant is unique because its flower consists of two contrasting colors (red and blue).

 A Fuchsia is a small plant or a bush which has pink, purple, or white flowers. the flowers hang downwards, with their outer petals curved backward.

#)HOW TO  TAKE CARE OF FUCHSIA?
                                         
  1. Water it more in summer and less in winter. If you water it as for its need it will grow abundantly pretty much all summer long.
  2. Make sure that no insects are taking over the leaves of the plant. The main part mostly where insects are found is in the area where the stem and leaf meet.
  3. These plants should get the right amount of light. They like the area where there is indirect sunlight. 
  4. During summer it weakens the fuchsia plant. So give this plant plenty of shade.
  5. Make sure there is adequate space for the drainage of water.

#) HOW TO PROPAGATE THIS PLANT?


 This plant is easy to propagate. At first, cut its stem about 2 to 4 inches (in spring season).  After that remove the second or third pair of leaves and keep it in water for about 3 to 4 weeks. You will see the cuttings have made good roots. After that keep it in the soil.
