Money Plant

Binayak Jha October 28, 2020

#)MONEY PLANT:

                                                              

                                             

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Epipremnum aureum

NORMAL NAME: PAISA BIRUWA

#)INTRODUCTION:

The plant is known as a money plant because its leaves are(round, flat, heart-shaped )coin-shaped. It can grow with the support of a stick and other hard materials. This young plant bear 3 to 4-inch long heart-shaped leaves. This plant is mainly grown indoors. Besides this money plant is believed to bring prosperity and lucks.

#)HOW TO TAKE CARE OF MONEY PLANT?

  1. Keep your money plant where there is indirect sunlight.
  2. Water it once a day.
  3. Water the money plant until the water comes out of the drainage hole.
  4. do not move your money plant often.

#) HOW TO PROPAGATE MONEY PLANT?

 Propagation of a money plant should be done in early spring. After you cut water the plant to prevent it from drying. Your plant will have roots and will be of 4 cm long before transplanting into a new pot.
